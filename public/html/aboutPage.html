Who am I


Kontawat Wisetpaitoon


Meet Kontawat Wisetpaitoon, the creative mind behind our handmade shop website from Phrae Province. As a student in web programming, Kontawat has utilized their skills and passion for web design to bring online shop to life. With a love for all things handmade, Kontawat has worked tirelessly to curate a selection of unique and high-quality items that showcase the talent of local artisans in Phrae Province, Kontawat is dedicated to sharing the beauty and culture of this region with the world through our online store. Whether you're a fan of handmade goods or simply looking for something special, we invite you to explore our selection and see for yourself the talent and artistry that Kontawat has brought to our online shop.
